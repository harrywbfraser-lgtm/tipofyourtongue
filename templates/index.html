🧠 Tip of Your Tongue

When you just cannot for the life of you remember a word... lethologica.

Tip of Your Tongue is a free AI-powered word finder built to help when a word is just out of reach. Describe what you mean — for example, “a word for fear of spiders” — and the tool suggests the most likely English word, plus alternatives and a definition. Ideal for writers, students, teachers, crossword fans, and English learners.
